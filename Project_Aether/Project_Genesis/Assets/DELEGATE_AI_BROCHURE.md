## The Problem

**68% of law firms have no workflow management system.** Partners delegate via email, voice, and sticky notes. The result:

| Impact | Cost |
|---|---|
| Lost tasks & forgotten delegations | 8-12% of billable hours vanish |
| No visibility into workload | Associates overwhelmed, partners blind |
| Manual tracking & follow-up | 2-3 hours/week per attorney wasted |

**For a 10-attorney firm billing $300/hour, that's $120K-$180K/year in lost revenue.**

## The Solution

**Delegate AI** transforms task delegation from a liability into a competitive advantage.

### 🎯 3-Click Intelligent Delegation

> *"Draft the Smith discovery response, assign to Sarah, due Friday, bill to matter 2024-0847."*

Delegate AI's AI engine **instantly**:
- ✅ Classifies the task (Discovery, Motion, Filing, etc.)
- ✅ Assigns priority and routes to the right associate
- ✅ Logs to the correct matter for billing
- ✅ Sends notifications via email or Slack
- ✅ Tracks completion and billable hours recovered

### 🔒 Attorney-Client Privilege — Guaranteed

Your client data **never touches** the AI model. Our **5-layer Privacy Shield** ensures:

1. **Data Minimization** — AI only sees task metadata (category, priority, deadline)
2. **AES-128 Encryption** — All documents encrypted at rest in the Compliance Vault
3. **Firm-Level Isolation** — Row-level security ensures no cross-firm data access
4. **Transport Security** — TLS 1.3, JWT authentication, rate limiting
5. **AI Restrictions** — No training on your data. Stateless inference. Human-in-the-loop always.

**✅ ABA Model Rule 1.6 Compliant | Privacy Shield Audit: CLEARED**
