## OUTREACH STRATEGY (CMO)

### Phase 1: Warm Introduction (Week 1-2)
1. **CEO** → Personal outreach to 3-5 firms via LinkedIn InMail or email
2. **Deep Crawler** → Compile managing partner names from firm websites + LinkedIn profiles
3. **CMO** → Draft 2 email templates: "Innovation Partner Invitation" + "AI Pilot Opportunity"

### Phase 2: Community Engagement (Week 2-4)
1. Submit guest post to LawSites (Bob Ambrogi) about "AI-Native Task Delegation"
2. JD Supra sponsored article placement ($500-1,000 from approved budget)
3. LinkedIn Ad campaign targeting: "Managing Partner" + "Solo Practitioner" + "Practice Manager" ($300-500)

### Phase 3: Conversion (Week 4-6)
1. Schedule 30-minute demo calls with interested firms
2. Sign Data Processing Agreements (DPA) — required by Compliance Officer
3. Onboard first 3 firms to pilot environment

### Success Metrics
- **Minimum:** 3 signed beta firms by Week 6
- **Target:** 5-7 signed beta firms by Week 6
- **Optimal:** 10 signed beta firms by Week 8
