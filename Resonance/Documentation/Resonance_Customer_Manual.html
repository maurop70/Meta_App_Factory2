Children with Auditory Processing Disorder (APD) hear the words — but the brain scrambles the meaning. Traditional tutoring fails because it assumes hearing equals understanding. One explanation is never enough.

Speech therapy sessions are expensive, limited to 30–60 minutes a week, and end when the session does. Your child's curiosity doesn't stop at the therapist's door.

Resonance fills the 167 other hours of the week — providing continuous, adaptive support that meets your child where they are, not where a curriculum says they should be.

Built for Real Learning, Not Just Answers

Every feature exists because a child with learning differences needed it.

🔄

Teach-Back Loop™

Alex never lectures. He explains a concept, gives keyword hints, and asks your child to teach it back. If they struggle, he rephrases with a new metaphor. This is how deep understanding forms — through active recall, not passive reading.

🗺️

Live Mind Maps

At any point, your child can generate a visual mind map of what they've been learning. Complex topics become clear, connected diagrams — turning abstract knowledge into something they can see and navigate.

📊

Council of Therapists

Behind the scenes, a multi-specialist AI council (Speech-Language Pathologist, Occupational Therapist, Educational Psychologist, Behavioral Analyst) collaborates to shape Alex's approach to your child's unique profile.

📄

Clinical Report Intelligence

Upload your child's IEP, speech evaluation, or psychological assessment. Resonance's AI reads and understands real clinical documents, then automatically adjusts Alex's strategies to match professional recommendations.

📸

Vision OCR — Photo Homework

Your child can snap a photo of a worksheet, textbook page, or whiteboard. Gemini Vision instantly reads the content and Alex jumps into guided tutoring — no typing required.

🧩

Adaptive User Profiling

Resonance passively learns your child's emotional patterns, academic strengths, and interests over time — without surveys or questionnaires. Alex naturally adjusts his approach session by session.
