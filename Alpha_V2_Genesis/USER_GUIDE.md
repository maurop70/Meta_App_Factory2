## 1. What Is Alpha V2 Genesis?

Alpha V2 Genesis is an **agentic trading intelligence system** designed to help you trade SPX Iron Condors systematically and with discipline. It combines:

- **Live market data** (SPX, VIX, IV Rank, HV, options chains via yfinance)
- **Cloud AI research** (N8N Genesis v3 workflows powered by Gemini)
- **V3 Streaming Chat** (SSE-powered conversational AI with context injection)
- **Local risk logic** (Loki Engine ‚Äî the "Lead Quant Architect")
- **Daily thesis auditing** (Strategy Ledger ‚Äî tracks whether your trade rationale still holds)
- **Multi-channel alerts** (Windows toast + mobile push via ntfy.sh)
- **Supabase Memory Engine** (persistent conversation history across sessions)
- **LangSmith Telemetry** (full trace observability for every LLM call)
- **Stability Suite** (7 built-in modules for resilience, monitoring, and auto-recovery)

The system does not place trades automatically. It **evaluates, recommends, and monitors** ‚Äî you make the final decision.

## 2. How to Launch

Double-click **Alpha V2 Genesis.lnk** on your desktop, or run:

```bat
launch.bat
```

This will:

1. **Preflight Check** ‚Äî validates .env keys, Python deps, N8N connectivity, and port availability. If any critical check fails, launch is aborted with clear error messages.
2. Kill any stale Python/Node processes
3. Start `server.py` (Flask + ngrok tunnel + V3 SSE streaming + graceful shutdown hook)
4. Initialize V3 Streaming Engine (`stream_bridge.py` ‚Äî Vault, LangSmith, Memory)
5. Activate N8N Alpha workflows (6 workflows)
6. Serve the React UI at `http://localhost:5173`
7. Open your browser automatically

**On shutdown** (closing the Vite window or pressing Ctrl+C):

- N8N workflows are automatically **deactivated** to prevent execution burn
- Even closing the terminal window (X button) triggers graceful deactivation

The **N8N Ledger Cron** fires automatically at **09:15 EST on weekdays** to refresh all data.

### 3.3 Strategic Regime (inside Loki Proposal)

**What it is:** The current market classification that determines *which Iron Condor strategy to deploy*.

| Regime | Meaning | Strategy Used |
|---|---|---|
| `STABLE` | IV Rank < 30, VIX moderate, SPX in tight range | **7 DTE Tactical** ‚Äî quick weekly premium, fast theta decay |
| `VOLATILE` | IV Rank ‚â• 30 or VIX elevated | **45 DTE Core Income** ‚Äî higher credit, wider buffers |

The regime is computed by Loki from live VIX, IV Rank (30d), and the spread between implied and historical volatility (IV-HV spread). A wide IV-HV spread means options are "expensive" relative to how much the market is actually moving ‚Äî the ideal condition for selling premium.

**How to use it:** Follow the recommended strategy. Don't override it unless you have a specific view. If VOLATILE, expect wider suggested strikes and lower probability of profit targets.

- **IV Window** ‚Äî Put IV% and Call IV% on the suggested strikes

**Entry Score** is a composite of 5 factors:

**Trade Rationale** ‚Äî the paragraph below the score is generated by the Strategy Ledger engine and explains *why* this specific trade makes sense given today's conditions. Save this for reference ‚Äî it becomes the baseline against which daily drift is measured.

### 3.6 Challenger Trade Scan

**What it is:** Every day, the system asks: *"If I were entering this trade TODAY from scratch, what would the optimal Iron Condor look like?"* It then compares that to your current position.

**Why it matters:** Markets shift. The "perfect" condor from 3 weeks ago may not be the perfect condor today. The challenger scan surfaces whether a better risk-adjusted alternative exists RIGHT NOW.

**Key fields:**

- **Challenger Strikes** ‚Äî the optimal strikes if entering today
- **Challenger Credit** ‚Äî what you'd receive for the new position
- **Cost to Roll** ‚Äî what it would cost to close your current position and open the challenger
- **Expected Move Match** ‚Äî how well the challenger covers the current MMM (market-maker move)
- **Pivot Warranted** ‚Äî YES or HOLD

**When to pivot:**
The system recommends pivoting only when the challenger's short strikes are materially wider AND the cost to roll is covered by the additional credit within 1 week. Otherwise, **HOLD** ‚Äî your current position has already absorbed decay and pivoting resets the clock.

### 3.8 Volatility Agent + N8N Forecast

**IV Rank (30d):** Measures how "expensive" options are right now relative to the past 30 days. Scale: 0‚Äì100%.

- `IVR > 50` ‚Üí Options are elevated ‚Üí More premium available ‚Üí Favorable for selling
- `IVR < 20` ‚Üí Options are cheap ‚Üí Less premium, smaller buffers ‚Üí Unfavorable

**HV (30d):** 30-day realized (historical) volatility of SPX. If IV Rank >> HV, the market is pricing in more fear than actually occurred ‚Äî the "volatility premium" that Iron Condor sellers collect.

**Signal:** `SELL` = favorable conditions to enter. `WAIT` = IV is too low or conditions not ideal.

**N8N Forecast Badge:**
The colored badge (`BULLISH` / `BEARISH` / `NEUTRAL`) is the **Cloud Brain's 7-day directional bias**, generated by Gemini via N8N research workflows (Genesis v3).

| Forecast | What It Means | Adjustment |
|---|---|---|
| BULLISH | Cloud Brain expects upward pressure | Widen the call buffer slightly |
| BEARISH | Cloud Brain expects downward pressure | Widen the put buffer slightly |
| NEUTRAL | No directional edge detected | Symmetric IC, no adjustment needed |

üü¢ = Live from N8N Genesis v3  
üî¥ = Cached / Gemini local fallback (still valid, just not live N8N)

## 4. Strategy Ledger (Bottom of Dashboard)

The Strategy Ledger is the most sophisticated section of the system. It runs every morning at **09:15 EST** and performs a full audit of your open position against the conditions that justified opening it.

### 4.1 Thesis Status Badge

The single most important indicator in the ledger.

| Status | Drift Score | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| üü¢ `THESIS INTACT` | +1 to +6 | Market conditions still validate the original trade rationale |
| ‚ö™ `THESIS NEUTRAL` | -1 to 0 | Minor drift ‚Äî monitor but no action needed |
| üü° `THESIS DRIFTING` | -2 | Two or more entry conditions have deteriorated |
| üî¥ `THESIS BROKEN` | ‚â§-3 | Trade rationale has fundamentally changed ‚Äî seriously consider closing or rolling |

**Drift Score** is computed from 6 daily factors:

1. VIX move since entry (+1 if stable, -1 if spiked >20%, -2 if spiked >40%)
2. SPX move since entry (+1 if within range, -1 if outside expected move)
3. IV-HV spread (+1 if maintained, -1 if collapsed)
4. Days to upcoming HIGH-impact event (-1 if event within 2 days)
5. P&L momentum (+1 if capturing theta, -1 if losing)
6. Time value remaining (+1 if >21 DTE, neutral if ‚â§21 DTE)

**When to act on THESIS BROKEN:**

- Close the position if you're at >30% maximum loss
- Roll to a better strike set (pivot) if the challenger scan shows a worthy alternative
- If P&L is still positive, consider closing early and waiting for a better entry

---

### 4.2 P&L Progress Bar

A visual representation of profit captured toward the **50% profit target**.

- The bar fills left-to-right as you capture more of the original credit
- The **yellow vertical marker at 50%** marks the standard profit-taking threshold
- **Standard protocol**: Close the position at 50% profit OR at 21 DTE, whichever comes first

**Why 50%?** Backtesting shows that the additional profit from holding past 50% (the remaining 50% of max profit) requires disproportionately long time exposure to gamma risk. Taking profit at 50% and re-entering a fresh position produces better risk-adjusted annual returns.

**Why 21 DTE?** Gamma risk increases sharply inside 21 days to expiration. The position requires more active management, slippage increases, and the edge from selling premium diminishes.

---

### 4.3 Greeks (Œ∏, ŒΩ, Œ¥)

Real Black-Scholes analytical Greeks computed from live bid/ask quotes and implied volatility.

| Greek | Symbol | What It Measures | Healthy Range for IC |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Theta** | Œ∏ | Daily time-decay income collected per contract | +$5 to +$30/day |
| **Vega** | ŒΩ | P&L change per 1 percentage point VIX move | Negative (short vega is expected for IC) |
| **Delta** | Œ¥ | Directional exposure per $1 SPX move | -$5 to +$5/pt (near neutral) |

**Theta** should be positive (working in your favor) ‚Äî your position decays toward profit each day.

**Vega** will be negative for a standard Iron Condor ‚Äî you lose money when VIX rises. This is the primary risk of the strategy. Vega exposure tells you exactly how much you'd lose in a volatility spike.

**Delta** near zero means the position is balanced. Large positive delta means you're implicitly betting on upside; large negative delta means you're bearish. The Challenger Scan will flag if your delta is significantly off-center and there's a more neutral alternative.

---

### 4.4 Theta Decay Curve

A mini SVG chart showing your **projected position value from today to expiry**.

- **Blue curved line** ‚Äî theoretical premium decay path (non-linear, accelerates near expiry)
- **Blue dot** ‚Äî current mark (your position's current value)
- **Yellow dashed horizontal line** ‚Äî 50% profit target
- **Grey dashed vertical line** ‚Äî 21 DTE exit window
- **Colored vertical lines** ‚Äî upcoming catalyst events (üî¥ = HIGH impact, üü° = MEDIUM)

The curve uses a power model (exponent 0.65) to capture the reality that theta accelerates in the final 3‚Äì4 weeks ‚Äî the gamma risk zone where positions need active management.

**How to read it:** If the blue dot is above the yellow dashed line (mark > 50% of credit), you haven't yet hit your profit target. As time passes and the dot moves right along the curve, your position converges toward zero premium = maximum profit capture.

---

### 4.5 Entry Rating, Challenger Scan, Next Catalyst

Three quick-reference indicators at the bottom of each position card:

**Entry Rating:** How good were the conditions when you originally entered?

- üü¢ STRONG ENTRY (score 4‚Äì5): Near-ideal conditions. High confidence the trade was well-timed.
- üü° GOOD ENTRY (score 3): Solid conditions with minor caveats.
- üü† MARGINAL (score 2): Acceptable but not ideal. Extra vigilance warranted.
- üî¥ POOR TIMING (score 0‚Äì1): Entry was premature. Learn from this for next time.

**Challenger Scan:**

- ‚úî HOLD ‚Äî Your current position is still the best or comparable to any alternative. No action needed.
- ‚ö†Ô∏è PIVOT ‚Äî A demonstrably better IC exists today. The pivot rationale detail shows cost to roll and net credit improvement.

**Next Catalyst:** The nearest upcoming HIGH or MEDIUM impact event. Format: `Event Name (T-Xd)` where X is days until the event. If a catalyst is within 2 days, the system applies a drift penalty and increases scrutiny.

## 10. V3 Streaming Chat Engine

The V3 engine adds a **real-time conversational AI** directly inside the dashboard. Instead of sending prompts through N8N webhooks and waiting for a response, the V3 chat streams Gemini responses **token-by-token** via Server-Sent Events (SSE).

### How It Works

1. **You type** a question or command in the chat bubble (bottom-right of the dashboard).
2. **Context injection** gathers your live dashboard state (SPX price, VIX, active trades, Greeks, P&L) and attaches it to the prompt.
3. **Local file ingestion** reads `strategy_ledger.py` and `market_memo.md` so the AI has full situational awareness.
4. **SSE streaming** sends the enriched prompt to Gemini 2.5 Flash and streams the response in real time.
5. **Supabase memory** persists the conversation so context carries across sessions.
6. **LangSmith** traces every call for debugging and performance monitoring.

### What You Can Ask

- "Should I roll my current Iron Condor?" (the AI sees your exact strikes, P&L, and Greeks)
- "What happens if VIX spikes to 30?" (the AI knows your current position)
- "Analyze today's macro calendar for my position" (reads upcoming events)
- Any freeform question ‚Äî the AI has full context of your live dashboard

### Key Files

| File | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| `stream_bridge.py` | SSE bridge: Gemini streaming + context + history |
| `memory_engine.py` | Supabase conversation persistence |
| `vault_client.py` | Encrypted secrets (GEMINI_API_KEY, LANGCHAIN_API_KEY) |
